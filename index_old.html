Where to stay?

Kungliga Borgen is located 5 minutes from the city center of Stockholm, so feel free to choose any location in Stockholm city center that you like.
We will stay at Villa Källhagen which is 5 minute walk from the venue.

Are kids welcome?

We love your kids but since we want a big party, we’ve decided to have a kid-free wedding. If you want to bring your kids to the Friday pre-party that is okey.

Parking at the venue?

Pre-party: The location is placed right by the subway, it’s easier to take public transport or taxi. If you want to drive there please reach out to us before and we will help finding a parking spot for you.
Wedding: There’s only room for a few cars at the venue, so please consider taking public transport or taxi to the location. If you need to bring your car please let us know before since the venue needs to register your car. Please note: All cars and taxis needs to stop at the bottom of the hill before the gates as it’s very hard to turn the car around at the venue.

Will you attend?

We are excited to share this special day with you and would love to know if you can join us.
